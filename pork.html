Pepper Pork Chops





Ingredients




1kg pork chops

4 teaspoons of ginger garlic paste

1 tablespoon vinegar

4 tablespoons oil

2 teaspoon chili powder

1 ½ tablespoon of pepper powder

salt to taste

3 green chilies

3 medium onions sliced






Procedure


1) Marinate the chops with ginger garlic paste, pepper powder, chili powder and salt.

2) Let it rest for one hour.

3) heat oil in a large pressure cooker and add onions and sliced green chilies and cook till translucent

4) Add the marinated chops and mix well

5) Simmer for few minutes

6) Put the lid on the pressure coocker and seal.

7) Cook till chops are done and gravy dries for 10-15 minutes.

8) Serve with mashed potatoes(optional)

9) For mashed potatoes boil 2-3 potatoes in water and mash it up while warm
