Apple Cake





Ingredients


200 grams of flour

200 grams of butter

100 grams of sugar

Pinch of salt

2 eggs beaten well

1 teaspoon of baking powder

2 apples peeles and chopped into tiny pieces







Procedure


1) Beat the butter and sugar together till fluffy.

2) Add the eggs, flour, salt, apple pieces and baking powder.

3) Mix well.

4) Pour the mixture in a greased baking dish.

5) Bake for 25 minutes at 180 degrees.

6) Place on a seperate plate when it cools down.

7) Serve with whipped cream.
