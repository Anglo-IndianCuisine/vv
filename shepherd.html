Shepherd's Pie





Ingredients


500gms Minced meat

2 large onions chopped

3 large potatoes mashed

1 table spoon of garlic paste

Butter

Grated cheese

Salt to taste

1 teaspoon of peri-peri(optional)






Procedure


1) Place the onions in a pan and cook till translucent

2) Place the mince, peri-peri(optional) and garlic paste in the same pan and cook for 10 minutes

3) Grease an ovenproof dish with butter and transfer meat into the dish

4) Spread the mince evenly which will make the first layer

5) Boil the potatoes (peel off the skin first) and mash it up

6) Spread it evenly on top of the cooked meat to form the second layer

7) Sprinkle grated cheese on top of the potatoes to create a 3rd layer.

8) Cook in a preheated oven at 350 degrees for about 14-15 minutes till the cheese melts and the potato becomes brown.
