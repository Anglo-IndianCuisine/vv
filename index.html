About me


Hi! Thank you for visiting my website. My name is Raul Ignatius. I was born in an Anglo-Indian family. I think i was born with the love and passion for cooking and that is the reason I started helping my mother in the kitchen ever since I was 5 years old. By the age of 12 I was able to cook alone and started experimenting with different dishes and distorted versions of famous recipes.

I have been creating and testing out my own recipes in Italian, American, French, Indian and Anglo-Indian Cuisnes. So far I have been happy with my trials. This got me a wealth of experience in cooking and creating recipes. The reason I have created this website is to share my recipes with the world and promote the Anglo-Indian cuisine which will revive the memories of the bygone era of colonial India. This is why in this Website you will find no ads or no paid endorsements. It's just me sharing my love for cooking and my creations with the world.
