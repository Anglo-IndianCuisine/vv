Mince Ball curry





Ingredients



Curry


3 large onions chopped

6 curry leaves

3 teaspoons of chilly powder

1 ½ teaspoons coriander powder

3 tablespoons ginger garlic paste

5 tablespoons of tomato puree

1 teaspoon of spice powder

Salt

3 Tablespoons oil

1 teaspoon of turmeric powder


Mice balls


½ kg minced meat of mutton or beef

½ teaspoon spice powder

3 green chillies chopped

3 tablespoons of coirander leaved chopped

Salt

½ teaspoon turmeric powder







Procedure


1) Heat oil in a large pan and fry onions till golden brown

2) Add ginger garlic and curry leaves and fry

3) Add chilly powder, coriander leaves, spice powder and turmeric and fry for a few minutes until oil seprates from the masala.

4) Add the tomato puree and salt and let simmer

5) Add one cup of water and let it boil.

6) Meanwhile mix the spice powder, salt, chopped green chillies, corriander leaves and turmeric powder with mince

7) Form small balls with the mince

8) When the water starts boiling in the pan add the mince balls slowly and cook for 20 monutes till gravy is a little more thick
