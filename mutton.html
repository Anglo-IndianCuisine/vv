Railway Mutton Curry





Ingredients


1kg Mutton or lamb(cut into medium size pieces)

5 peppercorns

4 big onion slices

3 pieces of cinnamon

2 cloves

2 cardamoms

10 curry leaves

6 red chillies(break into small bits)

2 teaspoons of chilly powder

2 teaspoons of ginger garlic paste

Salt

3 tablespoons of oil

3 tablespoons vinegar







Procedure


1) Wash the meat

2) Mix the meat with ginger garlic paste, salt and chilly powder.

3) Heat oil in a pan and fry the onions, curry leaves, red chilles and other spices till they become golden brown

4) Add meat and mix well

5) Fry for few minutes

6) Add the vinegar and 1 cup of water and cook meat on medium heat.

7) Keep frying until gravy becomes thick.
