Soft Tangzhong Bread





Ingredients



Tangzhong Starter

40 gr. All Purpose Flour

150 gr. Full/Whole Milk



Bread

560g All Purpose Flour

11g Instant Yeast

60g Fine Sugar

8g Salt

Tangzhong Starter

100g Unsalted Butter

1 Egg

140g Whole/Full Milk






Procedure


1) Take a sauce pan and place the 40g of All Purpose flour and milk in it.

2) On high heat which continuously until the consistency becomes thicker and more creamy.

3) Cover the sauce oan and set it aside to cool for one hour.

4) In a large bowl place the 560g of All Purpose flour, the sugar, the instant yeast, the salt, the egg, the butter, the milk and the tangzhong starter(after cooled).

5) Knead with hand together slowly for 3-4 minutes until it all comes together.

6) Once it all comes together knead faster and harder for 18-20 minutes.

7) Grab the dough together and push it inside downwards like so:






8)Once the dough becomes more smooth place the dough in a greased bowl.

9)Cover the bowl and make sure its air tight.

10)Let the dough rise and double in size.

11)Once the dough has risen divide it into 8 equal parts.

12)Make each of the 8 segments into smooth balls.

13)place the balls in a baking dish like this one:






14) Cover the baking dish and let it rise again.

15) Once the bread rises again brush some milk gently on the top of the bread.

16)Place in preheated oven of 180 degrees for 20 minutes.

17) Take the bread out of the oven and let it cool beofre taking it out.
