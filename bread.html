Bread and Butter Pudding





Ingredients


1/4 cup of butter

10 slices of white bread

1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg

1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon

1 ½ cup of milk

1/4 cup heavy cream

2 large eggs

4 tablespoons sugar(white)

1 teaspoon vanilla extract.


Topping

175g butter

115g butter can of condensed milk

115g brown sugar

3 table spoons of golden syrup

rock salt( to add saltiness to the caramel)







Procedure


1) Heat the oven 355 F /180 C.

2) Grease a one-liter dish with butter

3) Cut bread into half to form triangles and spread butter one side of each of the bread triangles.

4) Place the bread into the dishes to make it overlap with the butter side up.

5) Heat milk, the cinnamon, nutmeg powder and cream in a saucepan and do not let it boil.

6) Beat eggs with 3 tablespoons of sugar and add vanilla extract.

7) Pour the warm milk (make sure not hot) over the eggs and whisk continuously till all milk is added.

8) Pour the egg mixture slowly and evenly over the bread until all the liquid is added.

9) Press down the bread into the liquid and set aside for 30 minutes

10) Bake the pudding in the hot oven for 40 to 45 minutes until surface is golden brown and the pudding is well risen.

11) While the pudding is baking, we must use the time to make the caramel

12) Place butter in a sauce pan and let it heat. Then add the condensed milk and stir well.

13) Add the sugar and the golden syrup and keep mixing until you get the desired texture.

14) Take of the stove and add the rock salt and mix well.

15) Pour it on the bread and butter pudding.
