Traditional Christmas Cake





Ingredients


500 grams of refined flour

300 grams of soft brown sugar

1/4 teaspoon salt

2 teaspoons of cocoa powder

250 grams of cashews and almonds

500 grams butter

3 eggs beaten well

4 tablespoons milk

Â½ teaspoon baking powder







Procedure


1) Sift the flour, salt, baking powder into a large bowl.

2) Add the cocoa powder and brown sugar and mix well.

3) Add butter and mix with fork.

4) Add the beaten eggs and milk and mix well to give a stiff consistency.

5) Pour the mixture into a greased cake tin.

6) Smoothen the top.

7) Pour the warm milk (make sure not hot) over the eggs and whisk continuously till all milk is added.

8) Bake in a moderately hot oven for about one hour till the cake is cooked.
