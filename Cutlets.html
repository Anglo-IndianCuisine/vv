Anglo-Indian Cutlets





Ingredients


1kg Chicken/lamb mince

4 tablespoons of sunflower oil

2 medium onions chopped

1 tablespoon garlic paste

1 ½ teaspoon chili powder

1 teaspoon of turmeric

Salt to taste

2 large potatoes

3 cup of breadcrumbs

4 large eggs (beaten in a bowl)






Procedure


1) Add oil to a large pan on medium to high heat. Add the onions and cook till translucent.

2) Add the mince and stir it a few times. Set the heat to low and let it cook for 10 minutes and add the garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric and a teaspoon of salt to taste

3) While the mince is cooking boil the potatoes.

4) Set aside two bowls. In one bowl place the beaten eggs and in the other bowl place the breadcrumbs

5) Once the mince and the potatoes are done mash the potatoes up and combine a little potato with some mince together and form a nice neat ball(cutlet). Keep repeating this till all the mince meat and the potatoes are used.

6) Next dip one cutlet into the egg and then into the breadcrumbs. Keep repeating this for every ball.

7) Add oil to a pan and fry the cutlets till the breadcrumbs become golden brown.
